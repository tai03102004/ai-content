## ğŸ¯ Core Principles

### 1. People-First Content

- Write for humans, not search engines
- Provide genuine value and unique insights
- Answer questions comprehensively

### 2. E-E-A-T Framework

- **Experience**: Demonstrate firsthand experience with the topic
- **Expertise**: Show deep knowledge and understanding
- **Authoritativeness**: Establish credibility through citations, data, case studies
- **Trustworthiness**: Be transparent, accurate, and honest

### 3. Search Intent Satisfaction

- **Informational**: Provide comprehensive explanations, how-tos, guides
- **Transactional**: Help users make purchase decisions
- **Navigational**: Guide users to specific resources
- **Commercial**: Compare options, provide recommendations
